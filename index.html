The Four Modules

8 weeks. 4 modules. Built around the operator, not a video-watching schedule.

Each module is two weeks. Live group call plus office hours plus templates, scripts, and SOPs handed off as you complete each week. No video-on-demand library to fall behind on.

Module 1
Weeks 1-2

Sales, Pricing, and Estimating

The pricing structure that holds across residential and commercial work. The estimating process that does not depend on you being on site. The bid template that closes the right percentage of jobs at the right margin. If your closing rate on residentials is above 70%, you are priced too low.

Module 2
Weeks 3-4

Crew, Recruiting, and Retention

The sourcing channel that does not depend on word of mouth. The 90-day retention SOP. The reason most operators lose their second crew lead and how to not be one of them. The wrong second crew lead does more damage than the wrong second truck.

Module 3
Weeks 5-6

Ops, KPIs, and Software Install

Three numbers on the dashboard before the laptop opens. The weekly KPI cadence. The software install, your existing CRM or Home.works or whatever you are running, set up to feed the cadence and not the other way around. The software does not run the business. The cadence does.

Module 4
Weeks 7-8

Owner-Operator Transition

Stepping off the truck. Replacing yourself on estimating, then on operations, then on sales. The 90-day plan for the role you are stepping into next. The owner who replaces themselves on estimating earns the right to replace themselves on operations.

How It Runs

Live every week. No video-on-demand library to fall behind on.

01

Weekly Live Group Call

One live group call with Mike per week. 60-90 minutes. The content is the live call. Miss a call, you get the recording, you walk it. No video library to ignore.

02

Office Hours Twice a Month

1-1 questions on your specific operation. Route density, crew math, pricing structure, P and L review. The time to go deep on your own numbers, not a generic template answer.

03

Private Community Channel

Operator-only channel for the 8 weeks. Templates, scripts, SOPs, recruiting docs, and P and L worksheets handed off as you complete each module. No coaches in the room who have not run a route.

Who This Is For

The cohort is not for everyone. Here is who belongs in the room.

This Is The Right Room If
  • You are a sub-$1M home service owner-operator, still riding a mower three days a week
  • You are doing $150K-$700K a year and want out of the truck and into the office
  • You are crossing into systems at $500K-$2M, crew of 5-15, ops getting away from you
  • You are a career-changer moving into the trades in the next 6-12 months, deciding between starting independent or coming in through Augusta
  • You finished the free Landscape Business Course and want a live room, not another self-paced video
  • You run any home service trade: lawn, landscaping, HVAC, plumbing, pest, pressure washing, roofing
This Is Not The Right Room If
  • You are at $2M+ running 20+ crew. Your conversation is the Augusta franchise route or 1-1 mentorship, not the cohort.
  • You want a video library to watch at your own pace. The free courses on mikeandes.com are built exactly for that, and they are free.
  • You want a franchise business model. That conversation is at augustalawncareservices.com. Cohort grads who choose the franchise route get routed there.
  • You want Home.works software. That is at home.works. Cohort Module 3 walks the install if your CRM is Home.works; the cohort runs cleanly on whatever you are already on.
